SHOYU CHICKEN RAMEN

Cuisine Description

Looking for a meal that will warm both your soul and the senses?

Warm up with a bowl of inspiration and tradition brought together in our
Signature Shoyu Chicken Ramen! Succulent slices of juicy chicken
lightly seared for a crispy finish atop a fragrant soy-based broth. A touch
of tradition graces the bowl with a swirl of narutomaki and a crispy sheet of
nori, making each spoonful a taste of comfort and authentic Japanese cuisine.
